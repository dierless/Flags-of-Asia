Timor-Leste

Specifications

  • Local Name: Timor-Leste / Timór Lorosa'e
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Flag of Timor-Leste
  • Adopted: May 20, 2002

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: The struggle for national liberation
  • Black: Colonial oppression and hardships overcome
  • Yellow: The path toward independence and development
  • White: Peace, hope, and unity

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • White star: Light guiding the nation toward peace
  • Two triangles: Historical challenges and the journey toward self-determination

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is raised during national remembrance days honoring the independence struggle.
  • It is commonly displayed at community gatherings that emphasize unity and rebuilding the nation.

Description

  • The flag reflects Timor-Leste’s journey from struggle to independence, symbolizing resilience and national hope.
  • For many citizens, it represents sacrifice, unity, and commitment to shaping a peaceful future.
