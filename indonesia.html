Indonesia

Specifications

  • Local Name: Indonesia (Republik Indonesia)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: Sang Merah Putih
  • Adopted: August 17, 1945

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Human courage, physical life, and bravery
  • White: Purity, spiritual life, and honesty

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Simple bicolor design symbolizing balance between body and soul

Meaning / Special Display

  • Displayed widely during Independence Day celebrations on August 17 as a symbol of national unity.
  • Often flown in villages and cities to reflect patriotism and remembrance of the independence struggle.
