China

Specifications

  • Local Name: 中国 (Zhōngguó)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: Five Star Red Flag
  • Adopted: October 1, 1949

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red field: Revolution and the spirit of the Chinese people
  • Yellow stars: Brightness and unity

    • Shapes and Symbols:

  • One large star: Leadership and the guiding role of the nation
  • Four smaller stars: The unity of the people in support of the central authority
  • Stars arranged with a circular orientation that shows harmony between the people and the state

Meaning / Special Display

  • Flown during national celebrations, the flag represents unity and national pride.
  • The arrangement of the stars symbolizes cooperative harmony between the people and leadership.

Description

  • The flag reflects the identity of China as a unified nation built on shared purpose and collective strength.
  • For its citizens, it represents loyalty to the country, national stability, and pride in cultural heritage.
