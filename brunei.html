Brunei

Specifications

  • Local Name: بروني دارالسلام (Brunai DārusSalām)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Flag of Brunei
  • Adopted: September 29, 1959

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Yellow: The Sultan and the monarchy
  • White & Black Stripes: Senior ministers and noble officials

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Red crest: Government authority and national identity
  • Crescent: Islam as the state religion
  • Hands: Benevolence and protection

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is prominently displayed on royal buildings and during national ceremonies tied to the Sultan.
  • It signifies loyalty and respect toward the monarchy, especially during events like the Sultan’s birthday celebrations.

Description

  • The flag highlights Brunei’s identity as a monarchy rooted in Islamic tradition and royal authority.
  • Citizens view it as a symbol of stability, cultural continuity, and reverence for the Sultan.
