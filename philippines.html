The Philippines

Specifications

  • Local Name: Pilipinas (Republika ng Pilipinas)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Pambansang Watawat ng Pilipinas
  • Adopted: June 12, 1898

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Blue stripe: Peace, truth, and justice
  • Red stripe: Patriotism and valor
  • White triangle: Equality and fraternity
  • Yellow sun: Freedom, democracy, sovereignty
  • Three stars: The three main island groups: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Equilateral white triangle on the hoist side
  • Eight-rayed golden sun in the center of the triangle, representing the first eight provinces that revolted against Spain
  • Three five-pointed stars at each corner of the triangle

Meaning / Special Display

  • When the blue stripe is on top: Peaceful time
  • When the red stripe is on top: Time of war
