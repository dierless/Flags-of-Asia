Malaysia

Specifications

  • Local Name: Jalur Gemilang
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Jalur Gemilang (Stripes of Glory)
  • Adopted: September 16, 1963

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Courage and strength
  • White: Purity
  • Blue: Unity of the Malaysian people
  • Yellow: Royal color of the Malay rulers

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • 14 alternating red and white stripes: Equal status of the 13 states and the federal government
  • Blue canton with a yellow crescent and 14-point star: Islam as the official religion and unity among the states

Meaning / Special Display

  • Displayed during national holidays and official ceremonies to represent unity and sovereignty.
  • The flag is a symbol of Malaysia’s federal system and multicultural harmony.

Description

  • The Jalur Gemilang embodies Malaysia’s values of unity, strength, and respect for tradition.
  • For citizens, it represents national pride, multicultural cooperation, and loyalty to the federation.
