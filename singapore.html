Singapore

Specifications

  • Local Name: 新加坡 (Xīnjiāpō)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: Flag of Singapore
  • Adopted: December 3, 1959

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Universal brotherhood and equality of all people
  • White: Purity and virtue

    • Shapes/Symbols:

  • Crescent moon: A young nation on the rise
  • Five stars: Democracy, peace, progress, justice, and equality

Meaning / Special Display

  • Raised during National Day celebrations as a symbol of unity and national progress.
  • Displayed prominently across homes and public spaces in August, reflecting national pride.

Description

  • The flag reflects Singapore’s aspirations as a growing nation built on equality, justice, and progress.
  • Citizens see it as a symbol of multicultural harmony and shared national responsibility.
