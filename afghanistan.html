Afghanistan

Specifications

  • Local Name: افغانستان (Afghānistān)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Flag of the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan
  • Adopted: August 15, 2021

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • White field: Purity, peace, and Islamic faith
  • Black text: Represents religious authority and strength

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Shahada inscription: The Islamic declaration of faith

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is primarily religious in nature, focusing on faith and identity under Islamic governance.
  • It is used during national and religious events, representing devotion to Islamic principles.

Description

  • The flag highlights Afghanistan’s identity through religion and simplicity, reflecting a government built on Islamic faith.
  • For its citizens, the flag represents devotion, national identity, and the values of faith and resilience.
